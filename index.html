Technologist & Entrepreneur

Hello, world! My name is Samira and I am a junior at CCNY, studying computer science. I am the Founder and CEO of Carella, a startup created to help car owners find the car parts they need at the price they want. We want to empower car owners to make informative decisions about their car, and help them get connected to the right mechanics and sellers that will give them the best deals. Check out our landing page at carella.one!

I am very much a technology-oriented person. I am particularly interested in full-stack because I enjoy the visual aspect of front-end and like understanding how things work in the backend. I am also interested in learning more about data science and delving into computer science research.

I am passionate about CS education, and making it more accessible to underrepresented groups in tech. I currently moderate and teach for a great organization called TechGirlz that is dedicated to closing the gender gap by introducing middle school girls to CS. In my free time, I love to immerse myself in the amazing world of k-dramas :)
